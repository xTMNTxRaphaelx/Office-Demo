Digi - शिक्षा is a learning platform providing access to students to a wide range of e-resources supporting secondary and senior secondary Board curricula. These resources have been reviewed, mapped and made accessible for a more diverse learning experience. Some of the content has been developed in-house, while other has been curated from open source. Students may use the AV resources and assessment exercises as referral material that would supplement classroom learning.

Disclaimer

The Video contents offered by CBSE in its Digi-शिक्षा portal are freely shared contents of various agencies that are in the field of free online education. These video contents are meant to act as learning aid and may not necessarily be exactly mapped with the NCERT books. The focus is on personalized learning anytime, anywhere, free of cost. This platform gives link to various educational resources so as to provide easy navigation for the students saving their precious time. The CBSE does not claim the ownership of the content and copyright. Copyright shall be applicable as specified in the respective content uploaded here with the consent of the agencies concerned.
